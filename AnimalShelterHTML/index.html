Making sure every animal has a home

Pixy's Animal Rescue is an animal shelter located in Rochester, NY. Here at Pixy's we offer a wide range of animals which you can welcome into your family. Our goal is to provide safe and quality sheltering, food, care, and love to all animals until permanent homes are found. Together with our local community, we aim to improve the lives of animals.
