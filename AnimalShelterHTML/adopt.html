The Adoption Process

The Adoption process at Pixy's Animal Rescue is very smooth. We take the necsesary measures such as background checks on potential candidates. Please contact us when you want to begin the adoption process. The first step is filling out an adopton questionnaire. The second step is having an interview with one of our representatives. Then attending a meet and greet with your adopted animal while we finalize the adoption process.
